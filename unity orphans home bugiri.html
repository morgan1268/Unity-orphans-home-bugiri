Who We Are

Unity Orphans Home Bugiri is a grassroots Christian organization founded in 2012, located in the rural Bugiri District of Eastern Uganda. We currently provide full-time care, education, medical support, and emotional restoration to over 85 orphaned children who have lost one or both parents due to disease, poverty, and community crisis. Our vision is to raise a generation of responsible, empowered, and spiritually grounded young leaders. At Unity Orphans Home, every child belongs – they are fed, clothed, and given access to quality education and vocational skills. Our approach goes beyond basic needs: we emphasize holistic development through farming (sustainability), sports (teamwork & health), and Bible study (moral foundation). We work hand-in-hand with local leaders, volunteers, and international partners to break cycles of poverty.

Our Mission & Commitment

To transform the lives of orphaned children by providing a loving home, nutritious food, formal education, medical care, and spiritual mentorship — empowering them to become self-sufficient and community-focused citizens. We believe every child deserves a family, a future, and a chance to shine.

Uganda is home to an estimated 2.7 million orphans, many of whom live without basic shelter or guidance. Unity Orphans Home Bugiri actively intervenes, offering a family-style environment with dedicated housemothers, academic support, and skills training. Our long-term plan includes expanding our vocational training center, constructing a modern borehole, and building additional dormitories to welcome 50 more children by 2026. With your support, we can continue planting seeds of hope across Bugiri district. Our commitment extends beyond childhood: we mentor teenagers until they can pursue further education or sustainable trades.

Life-Changing Activities

Through farming, sports and faith, we nurture responsible and joyful children.

Sustainable Farming

Our 5-acre farm grows maize, beans, vegetables, and cassava, providing fresh food for the children and generating income through surplus sales. The kids learn permaculture, animal husbandry (goats & chickens), and responsible land stewardship. Farming teaches patience, teamwork, and self-reliance. We also run a small poultry project that supplies eggs for breakfast and raises money for school supplies. In 2024, we harvested over 2,500 kg of maize — making the home almost 60% food self-sufficient.

Sports & Recreation

Sports build character! Our children participate in football, athletics, netball, and volleyball. We partner with local teams and annual district tournaments to boost confidence and social integration. Sports also teach discipline, leadership, and healthy competition. Regular exercise reduces trauma and creates friendships. Unity United FC, our junior team, has won two inter-orphanage trophies, giving our kids immense pride and community recognition.

Bible Study & Discipleship

Every morning and evening, children gather for age-appropriate Bible lessons, worship, and prayer. We focus on values like forgiveness, generosity, and hope. Weekly memory verses and mentorship circles help them develop strong moral foundations. Through faith-based counseling, many children overcome past trauma. Our goal is to raise compassionate leaders who impact their communities with love and integrity.

🌱 How Farming Changes Lives

Through our farm-to-table program, children not only eat healthy meals but also gain practical agricultural skills. During planting season, older teens assist in plowing and weeding, learning modern agronomy techniques. The harvest directly supports the nutrition of 85+ children and also provides food to vulnerable families in the neighboring villages. We also run a small mushroom project and plan to introduce drip irrigation. Farming teaches work ethic and ensures food security, reducing dependency on external aid. In the words of our director, "our farm is our pantry and our classroom."

⚽ Sports for Resilience

Our sports coordinator holds weekly drills, friendly matches, and annual sports days. Girls and boys play together on mixed teams, breaking gender stereotypes. Through sports, we have seen shy children blossom into confident young athletes. In 2023, three of our alumni earned sports scholarships in regional high schools. The routine of exercise promotes mental well-being, reduces stress, and creates a family-like bond among participants.

📖 Bible Study Impact

Each weekend, we host a "Joyful Hour" with storytelling, drama, and scripture memorization. Many children who arrived broken and angry have found peace and purpose through these sessions. Bible study also provides an ethical framework for decision-making and fosters a supportive community where each child is valued. We invite local pastors and volunteers for special retreats and camps, enhancing spiritual growth.

How Your Support Changes Everything

Every donation helps provide 3 nutritious meals daily, school fees, uniforms, medical checkups, and maintenance of our farming and sports facilities. Our upcoming project: building a vocational training workshop for older teens (carpentry and tailoring). By partnering with Unity Orphans Home, you become part of their family story. We welcome monthly partners, corporate sponsors, and one-time gifts. With accountability reports shared quarterly, you'll see the direct impact.
